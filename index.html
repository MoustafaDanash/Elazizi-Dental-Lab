📋 Payment Terms Guide

On Delivery — Doctor pays when you deliver the case.
On Pickup — Doctor pays when they come to collect.
Net 7 — Full payment due within 7 days of order date.
Net 15 — Full payment due within 15 days.
Net 30 — Full payment due within 30 days.
Weekly — Doctor settles balance every week.
Monthly — Doctor settles balance at end of month.
Credit — Custom credit limit set per doctor. System warns when limit is near.
